Frequently Asked Questions

help_outline How do I start a new sale?
Navigate to the Point of Sale page from the main menu. Select products by clicking on them or using the search bar. Add items to the cart, then proceed to payment. You can accept cash, mobile money, card payments, or insurance claims.
help_outline How do I add a new patient?
Go to the Patients page and click "Add New Patient". Fill in the required information including name, contact details, and medical history. The system will automatically generate a unique patient ID.
help_outline How do I manage inventory?
Use the Quick Inventory page to view current stock levels. You can search for products, update stock quantities, and add new products. The system will alert you when items are running low on stock.
help_outline How do I handle shift changes?
Use the Shift Management page to start and end shifts. The system tracks all transactions during your shift and provides a summary when you end your shift. Make sure to hand over properly to the next cashier.
help_outline How do I process mobile money payments?
Select "Mobile Money" as the payment method in the POS. Enter the customer's phone number and the amount. The system will simulate the mobile money transaction process for MTN, Airtel, or Zamtel.
help_outline How do I view sales reports?
Navigate to the Reports page to view daily, weekly, and monthly sales data. You can filter by date range, view top-selling products, and analyze payment methods. Reports can be exported or printed.
