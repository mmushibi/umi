## General Questions

### Q: What is UmiHealth?
A: UmiHealth is a comprehensive pharmacy management system designed to streamline pharmacy operations, improve patient care, ensure regulatory compliance, and enhance business efficiency through digital transformation.

### Q: Who can use UmiHealth?
A: UmiHealth is designed for:
- Pharmacy owners and managers
- Licensed pharmacists
- Pharmacy cashiers
- Operations staff
- System administrators
- Healthcare providers (with appropriate access)

### Q: What are the main benefits of UmiHealth?
A: Key benefits include:
- Improved operational efficiency
- Enhanced patient safety
- Regulatory compliance
- Real-time inventory management
- Comprehensive reporting
- Mobile accessibility
- Data security and backup

### Q: Is UmiHealth compliant with ZAMRA and ZRA regulations?
A: Yes, UmiHealth is designed to meet all ZAMRA (Zambia Medicines Regulatory Authority) and ZRA (Zambia Revenue Authority) requirements, including prescription tracking, inventory management, and tax compliance.

### Q: Can UmiHealth be used for multiple pharmacy branches?
A: Yes, UmiHealth supports multi-branch operations with centralized management, inter-branch stock transfers, and consolidated reporting.

## User Accounts and Access

### Q: How do I get a UmiHealth account?
A: Account creation process:
1. Contact your system administrator
2. Complete registration form
3. Provide professional credentials
4. Verify email/phone number
5. Set up security questions
6. Complete orientation training

### Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Password reset steps:
1. Click "Forgot Password" on login page
2. Enter your email/username
3. Check email for reset link
4. Create new password
5. Login with new credentials
6. Update security information

### Q: Can I change my username?
A: Usernames typically cannot be changed after account creation. Contact your system administrator for special circumstances.

### Q: How do I enable two-factor authentication?
A: Two-factor authentication setup:
1. Login to your account
2. Go to Settings → Security
3. Click "Enable 2FA"
4. Scan QR code with authenticator app
5. Enter verification code
6. Save backup codes

### Q: What should I do if my account is locked?
A: Account lockout resolution:
1. Wait for automatic unlock (typically 15 minutes)
2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset
3. Contact system administrator
4. Verify no unauthorized access attempts

### Q: Can I access UmiHealth from multiple devices?
A: Yes, you can access from multiple devices, but concurrent sessions may be limited based on your role and security settings.

## Prescriptions and Patient Care

### Q: How do I process a new prescription?
A: Prescription processing steps:
1. Search or register patient
2. Enter prescription details
3. Verify prescriber information
4. Check for drug interactions
5. Verify insurance coverage
6. Process payment if applicable
7. Dispense medication
8. Provide patient counseling
9. Generate receipt

### Q: What happens if a drug interaction is detected?
A: Drug interaction protocol:
1. System displays interaction details
2. Review severity level (Major/Moderate/Minor)
3. Consider alternative medications
4. Consult prescriber if needed
5. Document clinical decision
6. Proceed with appropriate safeguards

### Q: How do I handle prescription refills?
A: Refill processing:
1. Search for original prescription
2. Verify refill eligibility
3. Check remaining refills
4. Verify prescription validity
5. Process refill request
6. Update prescription status
7. Notify patient when ready

### Q: Can I access patient medication history?
A: Yes, authorized users can view:
- Current medications
- Past prescriptions
- Allergy information
- Adverse reactions
- Compliance history
- Clinical notes

### Q: How are patient allergies managed?
A: Allergy management:
1. Document all known allergies
2. Set allergy severity levels
3. Configure allergy alerts
4. Review allergies before dispensing
5. Update allergy information as needed
6. Maintain allergy history

## Inventory and Operations

### Q: How do I check stock levels?
A: Stock level checking:
1. Navigate to Inventory
2. Search for specific product
3. View current stock quantity
4. Check reorder point
5. Review location information
6. View expiry dates

### Q: What should I do when stock is low?
A: Low stock procedures:
1. Check reorder point settings
2. Create purchase order
3. Consider stock transfers from other branches
4. Notify customers of delays
5. Expedite delivery if possible

### Q: How do I process a stock transfer?
A: Stock transfer process:
1. Navigate to Transfers
2. Select source and destination branches
3. Choose products to transfer
4. Enter quantities
5. Arrange transportation
6. Generate transfer documentation
7. Confirm receipt at destination
8. Update inventory records

### Q: How are expired products handled?
A: Expiry management:
1. System alerts before expiry
2. Quarantine expired products
3. Document expiry details
4. Process returns to suppliers
5. Write off if necessary
6. Update inventory records
7. Generate expiry reports

### Q: Can I track product batches?
A: Yes, batch tracking includes:
- Batch number recording
- Expiry date tracking
- Supplier information
- Quality control results
- Recall capability

## Payments and Billing

### Q: What payment methods are supported?
A: Supported payment methods:
- Cash
- Mobile Money (MTN, Airtel, Zamtel)
- Credit/Debit Cards
- Insurance claims
- Store credit
- Gift cards

### Q: How do I process a mobile money payment?
A: Mobile money processing:
1. Select mobile money option
2. Choose provider (MTN/Airtel/Zamtel)
3. Enter customer phone number
4. Enter payment amount
5. Generate payment request
6. Wait for customer confirmation
7. Complete transaction

### Q: How are insurance claims processed?
A: Insurance claim processing:
1. Verify patient insurance coverage
2. Check medication eligibility
3. Calculate copayment amount
4. Submit electronic claim
5. Process patient portion
6. Generate insurance receipt
7. Document claim details

### Q: Can customers pay later?
A: Credit options depend on:
- Customer credit status
- Pharmacy credit policies
- Management approval
- Credit limit availability
- Payment terms agreement

### Q: How do I handle refunds?
A: Refund processing:
1. Locate original transaction
2. Verify refund eligibility
3. Select refund method
4. Enter refund amount
5. Process refund
6. Generate refund receipt
7. Update inventory if applicable

## Technical Issues

### Q: The system is running slowly. What should I do?
A: Performance troubleshooting:
1. Check internet connection speed
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Close unnecessary applications
5. Check system status page
6. Contact IT support if issue persists

### Q: I can't login to the system. What's wrong?
A: Login troubleshooting:
1. Verify correct username/password
2. Check Caps Lock status
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Check if account is locked
6. Verify system maintenance status

### Q: Why am I getting "Access Denied" errors?
A: Access denied causes:
- Insufficient user permissions
- Incorrect role assignment
- Session timeout
- IP address restrictions
- Security policy violations

### Q: What should I do during system maintenance?
A: Maintenance procedures:
1. Save all work in progress
2. Log out of the system
3. Wait for maintenance completion
4. Check status page for updates
5. Log in after maintenance ends
6. Verify data integrity

### Q: How do I report technical issues?
A: Issue reporting:
1. Document error messages
2. Take screenshots if possible
3. Note steps to reproduce
4. Contact technical support
5. Provide system information
6. Follow up on resolution

## Security and Compliance

### Q: How is my data protected?
A: Data security measures:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Secure password storage
- Role-based access controls
- Audit logging
- Regular security updates
- Compliance with data protection laws

1. Change your password immediately
2. Report to security team
3. Review account activity
4. Enable additional security measures
5. Monitor for suspicious activity
